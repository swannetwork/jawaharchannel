This album is truly unique. It is very difficult to write a review of THAT album. This is something unusual from the beginning to the end. Really. I’ve already listened to many CDs in my life but this is completely different. This album is really intriguing, it has something elusive. It is both typical and not for this artist. Certainly it is an integral part of the discography of this artist. Maybe this is his most important work. It is quite possible.

There are many different albums. Some are good, some are bad. And some are totally bland. You know, you listen to it and nothing at all stays in your memory. Complete emptiness, a form without content. Like latin “lorem ipsum” sentence. Do you know it? It goes something like this:
